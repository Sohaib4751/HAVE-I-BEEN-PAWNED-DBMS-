About Us

Welcome to Leak Peak! We help you stay secure by checking if your email has been part of any data breaches. Stay informed, stay safe!

At Leak Peak, we monitor known data breaches across the internet to alert you if your personal information has been exposed. By checking your email against our database, you can ensure your security and take action before any malicious actors gain access to your sensitive data. Our service is fast, reliable, and free, offering peace of mind in an increasingly digital world.

Whether you're an individual concerned about your privacy or a business owner wanting to protect your customers' data, Leak Peak has the tools you need to stay ahead of security threats. Join us in making the internet a safer place for everyone!
